# Roller stack Rev A

| Station | Roller | Diameter | Y position | Function |
|---|---|---:|---:|---|
| Pickup | pickup roller | 18 mm | 25 mm | pulls top sheet from tray |
| Registration | registration roller | 12 mm | 112 mm | deskews leading edge and releases on timing command |
| Process | OPC drum | 30 mm | 180 mm transfer tangent | carries latent image and toner image |
| Process | primary charge roller | 10 mm | above drum | charges OPC surface |
| Process | developer roller | 16 mm | before transfer | presents toner to latent image |
| Transfer | transfer roller | 14 mm | 180 mm | transfers toner from drum to paper |
| Fuser | hot roller | 24 mm | 255-285 mm zone | melts toner |
| Fuser | pressure roller | 24 mm | 255-285 mm zone | presses paper into hot roller |
| Output | exit roller | 16 mm | 360 mm | moves sheet to output tray |
